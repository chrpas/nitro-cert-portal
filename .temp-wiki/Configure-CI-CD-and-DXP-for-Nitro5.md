This guide details the steps for configuring CI/CD and a new DXP environment for Nitro5.

1. [Migrate DXP project](https://github.com/avensia/nitro5/wiki/migrate-dxp-project)
1. [Set up TeamCity](https://github.com/avensia/nitro5/wiki/Setting-up-TeamCity) and [Build during upgrade](https://github.com/avensia/nitro5/wiki/Build-during-upgrade)
1. [Set up Octopus Deploy](https://github.com/avensia/nitro5/wiki/Setting-up-Octopus-Deploy)
1. [Configure host names](https://github.com/avensia/nitro5/wiki/Setting-up-DXP#step-5-dns), usually with a -nitro5 suffix, e.g. preprod-nitro5.myproject.com
1. Do first deploy (i.e. trigger "Continuous deploy" in TeamCity)
