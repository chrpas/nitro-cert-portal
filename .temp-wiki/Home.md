Nitro5 is Avensia's starter site for [Optimizely Customized Commerce](https://www.optimizely.com/products/commerce/b2c/) and [Optimizely Content Cloud](https://www.optimizely.com/products/content/). It is a major technical upgrade of Nitro to [ASP.NET Core](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/aspnet/core/introduction-to-aspnet-core?view=aspnetcore-8.0).
