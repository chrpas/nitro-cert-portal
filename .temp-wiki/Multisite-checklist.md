Things to consider before going multi-market, in no particular order.

* Currencies
    * See also prices.
* Tax rules
    * Vat rates for category of goods.
    * State taxes in US.
* Addresses
    * Titles in germany
    * Regions / States
    * Formatting of addresses
        * Zip codes are different, spacing and capitalization.
    * Length of addresses
    * Specific parts of country that has different rules
        * Jersey (UK)
        * Lesbos (Greece)
        * Madeira (Portugal)
* Product catalog requirements
    * Export rules
        * Market specific restrictions
            * Temporary ban / embargo / war
            * Import rules for specific country
            * ( Yes, these things happen, point in case, someone decided to introduce Russia as war broke out as a good idea to make profit.)
        * Country within / outside EU (norway)
    * Descriptions
    * Content declaration
* Return policies
    * Germany has stricter 
* Languages
    * Multi-lingual ( language selector )
        * Within market?
    * Arabic ( right to left writing )
    * Translations
        * Content 
        * Products
        * Code-Translations
        * Third party components
* Prices
    * Country or market specific?
    * Adjustable prices
        * Quantity of prices / customer specific prices.
* Campaigns
    * Country or market specific?
* CRM
    * Market based
* Top level domain structure
    * Multiple TLD:s or a single .com market
    * Sitemaps
    * New TLD:s require new SSL certificates
* SEO, old a new.
    * Often related to TLD
* Product search
    * Suffixes are way more important in Finland.
* Shipping providers
    * Does your provider work in this country
    * Popularity of said shipping provider.
    * Do we need multiple psp
        * Backup provider?
* Payment providers
    * Does your current PSP work in this country
    * Is it a preferred payment sort etc.
    * Risks of prepaid goods.
    * Do we need multiple psp
        * Backup provider?
* Geo location
    * Is geo location enough?
    * Landing pages?
    * Automatic redirection?
        * SE => .se
        * DE => .de
* Bots
* Cultures
    * Formatting rules for numbers ( comma, decimal)
    * Formatting rules for currencies $10 vs 100 SEK, or 100 ,- NOK.
    * Formatting rules for Dates.
* Time-zones
    * Such as campaigns go-live date.
* WCAG rules
    * Norway being more stringent than EU (for now)
* GTM / Analytics / GA4
    * How to trace purchase for a product
* Widgets
    * Pricerunner
    * Säker ehandel etc.
* Back office changes
    * Are all customer service representatives based in the original country
    * Are they placed outside of say sweden
